## Tellurium

**Tellurium** is a lightweight, highly customizable quality-of-life mod designed to improve clarity, awareness, and control without cluttering your screen. Whether you're playing competitively or just want a cleaner experience, Tellurium gives you the tools to stay informed and in control.

---

### ✨ Features

#### 🧠 Smart Indicators

Stay aware of important situations with clean, minimal indicators:

* **Low Health** alert (under 3 hearts)
* **Low Armor** durability warning
* **Burning** indicator
* **Elytra equipped** indicator
* **Shield stun** warning
* **Repot reminder** (when strength/speed are about to expire)
* **Totem warning** when not in offhand
* **Mace slow-fall** indicator

All indicators support **custom size and screen offset** for perfect positioning.

---

#### ⚔️ Combat & Feedback

* **3-block hit sound** for accurate reach feedback
* **Remove death animations** for clearer visuals
* **Flexible enchantment level display**

  * Vanilla
  * Decimal
  * Roman
  * Both

---

#### 📦 Hitbox Customization

Modify how hitboxes are rendered:

* Conditional hitboxes (speed-based, crawling/swimming, elytra use)
* Custom colors and toggles for:

  * Passive mobs
  * Neutral mobs
  * Hostile mobs
  * Players
 
* Option to hide:

  * Eye boxes
  * Look vectors
 
* F3 + B state stored between sessions
* Custom thickness

---

#### 🎮 Controls & Movement

* **Inverted sprinting** option
* **GUI sneaking** (stay sneaking while opening menus)

---

#### 💬 UI & Quality of Life

* Adjustable **chat textbox opacity**
* **Store chat messages** when closing chat

---

#### 🎨 Full Customization

* Full **RGBA color control** (including opacity)
* Adjustable indicator **size and position**
* Dedicated configuration menus for hitboxes and indicators

---

### ⚙️ Configuration

Access everything through the in-game **Tellurium Configuration** screen made with **Ukulib**
